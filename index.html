Introduction

I am a motivated, hard-working individual and love to tackle challenging problems through developing a structured approach. I have a blend of engineering, Research, healthcare, data science as well as hundreds of hours of stakeholder engagement, managing project timelines, maintaining product backlogs and opportunity sizing expereinces. I have worked prior as a statistical consultant on design of experiments and statistical testing to clinicans at Weill Cornell and as a point lead for Clinical Informatics at a fast paced startup- Sema4 for launching patient encounter timeline apps for clinicains, succesfully decreasing their time spent on collecting patient history by 15-20 minutes per patients especially for Immuno and Targeted Therapy groups. As a Data scientist at CVS, I am the owner of launching cost savings and increasing HEDIS quality dollar health product campaigns. Following Scrum and Agile principles in daily standups, I regularly work with marketing, legal, clinical teams and engineering for rapid iterations in our ongoing campaigns. My target areas include Hospital Readmissions, Care Managemnet warming and improvement, increasing Rx-Adherence, sizing Covid Risk for Medicaid population and preventing lapse of important Cardiovascular and Diabetes appointments due to Covid. We have succesfully decreased Hospital Readmissions rate and increased pharmacy fill rates and improved medication Adherence (in terms of PDC). My core work focusses on opportunity sizing and calculation of ROI generally for next 2-5 years, from targeting high risk members and encouraging healthy behavior habits through optimum messaging along with marketing and legal. Priortizing hypothesis to be tested along important OKR's, deciding right metrics for evaluation, building a statistically sound design of experiment, Profiling members and building machine learning risk models are some of my day to day activities. I am well versed on using the latest SFMC architecture for go to market channels, Amazon Web Services (AWS) for Big Data Handling, Optimizely for Design of Experiment and am proficient at using Python, R, Tableau.
